Cinnamon Roll Recipe

A cinnamon bun, also known as a cinnamon roll, is a delectable pastry characterized by its sweet and aromatic flavor profile. Composed of a soft and fluffy yeast dough, the bun is generously layered with a mixture of cinnamon, brown sugar, and butter, which contributes to its irresistible taste. The dough is rolled into a spiral shape, creating distinct layers that puff up during baking, resulting in a tender and pillowy texture.

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup warm milk (110°F/43°C)
  • 1/4 cup warm water (110°F/43°C)
  • 1 large egg
  • For the Filling:
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together melted butter, warm milk, warm water, and egg.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  7. Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a 16x21-inch rectangle.
  8. Spread the softened butter over the dough, then sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  9. Roll up the dough tightly and cut into 12 even slices.
  10. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  12. While the rolls are baking, prepare a simple glaze by mixing powdered sugar with a small amount of milk or water.
  13. Drizzle the glaze over the warm cinnamon rolls and serve. Enjoy your delicious homemade cinnamon rolls!
